Welcome to Dsigner Studio

Since 2009
10 Years+ Experience

We are a full service creative agency focused on Exhibitions & Interior Designing. We have a core team of professionally qualified staff and a fleet of production staff which form the central force for stall designing & project management. With special focus in stall designing & 3d visualization, residential and commercial interiors, our master planning, architecture and design services have expanded to – mixed used scheme, interior & exterior designing, café & restaurants, terrace gardens & landscaping.

Dsigner Studio has the capability to design your DREAM PROJECT. We intermingle style, substance, sophistication & serenity in all OUR work. Dsigner Studio is located in Gurgaon where you can personally visit to discuss your requirements..
